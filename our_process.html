Reliant Process

  • 1.    Receiving with quality check
    Reliant values long term customer relationships. By making sure our equipment physically looks good, we ensure we can pass this quality standard and feeling of pride to our customers.
  • 2.    Prep and install following manufacturer documentation
    There is a right way and wrong way to do things. By doing things the right way, with proven processes, we ensure our customers will get the results they expect from their order.
  • 3.    Power-up sequence and monitor
    Our customers deserve a flawless customer experience. The Power-Up phase tells us what hidden issues may exist that need correction or possibly to remove the equipment from sellable inventory.
  • 4.    Burn-in - Run time counter initiated
    Time will tell us if this equipment has passed our quality standards, ensuring our team can count on the equipment to deliver results for the customer.
  • 5.    Data Erasure as required initiate and analyze sanitization process
    We value our customers privacy and regulatory requirements, which leads to higher levels of trust and teamwork.

    Our Customers deserve to install the equipment the moment it arrives, freeing up our team to help other customers vs. chasing down missing components.

    Review output for Reliant's Rock Certified qualification is the final stamp of approval guaranteeing a positive customer experience
